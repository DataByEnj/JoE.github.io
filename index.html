About Me

Welcome! I’m a data enthusiast driven by curiosity and a passion for uncovering insights through data. My journey began with a deep interest in cleaning and organizing raw data in Excel, which quickly evolved into a fascination with turning complex datasets into meaningful, actionable stories. This passion for data transformation led me to explore SQL, where I am working to deepen my expertise in managing and analyzing large datasets. I’m excited about the potential to uncover insights that guide strategic decisions and create growth opportunities for organizations.
In my experience across the nonprofit and higher education sectors, I’ve consistently used data to help teams achieve their goals. For instance, I leveraged Excel to analyze recruitment trends, track progress against targets, and identify performance gaps. By cleaning and organizing student intake data, I built dashboards and visualizations that provided actionable insights, enabling teams to adjust strategies and successfully reach their objectives. As I continue to grow my Excel, SQL, and Tableau skills, I am committed to using data to make a positive impact. As a Data Analyst, I look forward to contributing to data-driven projects that help organizations achieve their goals.
